Who is a Good Human?

A good human being possesses qualities such as kindness, empathy, integrity, compassion, and humility. They treat others with respect, show understanding and support, and act selflessly for the well-being of others. They are honest, trustworthy, and stand up for what is right.

Be a good human being, a warmhearted, affectionate person. That is my fundamental belief.

Dalai Lama
